A KubeCon + CloudNativeCon Europe roundtable discussion explored how cloud native principles enable production AI, with panelists from AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft, and solo.io emphasizing vendor-neutral infrastructure and platform maturity. The conversation covered key challenges including scaling AI workloads, Kubernetes evolution through Pod Groups and Dynamic Resource Allocation, and securing AI supply chains through open standards and consistent evaluation frameworks. The panelists agreed that community-driven, integrated security for autonomous agents is essential for production-ready AI deployments.
